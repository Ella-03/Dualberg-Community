About Us:


The White Lodge and its opposing counterpart, the Black Lodge, originated in ancient legends passed down by the Nez Perce tribes who once inhabited the Twin Peaks region. In the stories, the White Lodge was the home of the spirits who ruled over man and nature there. It was a place of great goodness, which could be accessed through strong feelings of love.
