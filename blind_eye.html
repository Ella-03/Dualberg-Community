About Us:


Our whole existance is to stop the folks in this town from knowing the scary existances that happen in Gravity Falls. Everyone that is part of our Society has seen things that will change your life FOREVER! The leader of our cult, Blind Ivan, wipes all memories that the folks might get to help them live their, normal, everyday lives.
